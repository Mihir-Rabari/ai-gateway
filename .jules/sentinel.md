## 2024-05-18 - [MEDIUM] Fix error logging and remove info leakage
**Vulnerability:** Fastify routes in `apps/api` were manually serializing `err.message` and `err.stack` and exposing them through generic `fastify.log.error` calls. Additionally, route handlers were inconsistently using global logging methods instead of request-scoped loggers, breaking trace correlation.
**Learning:** `errorHandlerPlugin` from `@ai-gateway/utils` wasn't consistently registered everywhere, leading to manual error handling in route controllers that leaked too much information into internal logs and inconsistently formatted 500 errors.
**Prevention:** Register `errorHandlerPlugin` at the root of every Fastify service and rely exclusively on request-scoped logging (`req.log.error`) in route handlers. Do not extract or return internal error details in API responses.
